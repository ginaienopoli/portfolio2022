About

Photographer & Retoucher

Gina Ienopoli is a commercial photographer specializing in home goods and portraits in Queens, New York. Being very detailed and creative in conceptual and problem solving shows her skill and style in her profession.

She has received her Associate's in Illustration, and Bachelor's in Animation and Interactive Media at The Fashion Institute of Technology. Gina's work has been in galleries internationally, and has won several awards. Her photo has been exhibited in London for Sony World Photography Awards, and her photography series was a Semifinalist for Adobe Design Achievement Awards.
